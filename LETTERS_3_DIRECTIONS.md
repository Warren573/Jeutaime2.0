# DIRECTION A: Warm Journal / Moleskine

## Emotional Tone
**Intimate diary, handwritten letters, nostalgic correspondence**
*Like opening a leather-bound journal of love letters*

---

## Spacing
- Top padding: 20px (breathing room)
- Between letters: 16px (intimate distance)
- Letter card padding: 20px (cozy)
- Line height: 1.8 (readable, airy)

## Letter Card Style
- Background: Off-white with subtle paper texture (#FAF8F3)
- Border: Soft brown 1px (#D4B896)
- Shadow: Soft drop shadow (warm sepia tone)
- Corners: Slightly curved, worn edge feeling
- Font: Serif for handwriting effect (Georgia, Garamond italic)
- Decorative: Top border with 〰️ or 📄 symbol

## Reveal Placement
**If level 3 photo unlocked:**
- Appears between last message and input
- Bordered like a polaroid photo
- Caption underneath: "📷 Photo de Sophie"
- Slight sepia tone overlay option

## Actions Placement
- Centered, with space between
- Icons large, labels underneath
- Styled as hand-drawn buttons
- Color: Warm gold (#D4A862)

## Color Palette
- Background: #FFF8F0 (warm cream)
- Text: #2C1A0E (dark brown)
- Accent: #D4A862 (warm gold)
- Secondary: #B8956A (muted brown)
- Paper: #FAF8F3 (off-white with texture)

## Overall Feeling
📖 **Personal diary, vintage correspondence, nostalgic, warm, intimate**

## Spacing

- Larger than messages (focal point)
- Gold frame/border (like a portrait)

- Subtle glow effect around photo

- Grouped, romantic labels: "💌 Write Back", "👥 Get to Know"
- Icons large, warm
- Gold accent color
- Hover effect: slight glow or lift

- Accent: #D4A862 (gold, romantic)
- Secondary: #C9A878 (muted gold)
- Seal/Lock: #7A1A1A (deep burgundy for mystery)

## Overall Feeling
💌 **Secret correspondence, romantic mystery, anticipation, reveal moment, intimate climax**

## Spacing
- Vertical gap between messages: 32-48px (breathing room, silence)
- Message padding: 0px (no card container)
- Line height: 1.6 (readable, airy)
- Overall: maximum whitespace, minimum containers

## Letter Card Style
- NO card container (just text)
- NO border, NO shadow
- NO background color
- Just text on white
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Inter, -apple-system)
- Alignment: Natural (left for some, centered for others)
- Signature: Small, light gray, right-aligned

## Reveal Placement
**Photo appears naturally:**
- Between messages and input
- No frame, no border, no decoration
- Just the photo with quiet caption below
- Seamless integration into message flow

## Actions Placement
- Minimal, centered
- Text only, no icons
- Light gray color
- Hover: subtle underline
- Single action: "[👤 Sophie's profile]" simple link

## Color Palette
- Background: #FFFFFF (pure white)
- Text: #2C1A0E (dark, readable)
- Secondary text: #8B6F47 (gray, quiet)
- Accent: #D4A862 (subtle, restrained)
- Border: #E8D9C6 (very light, almost invisible)

## Overall Feeling
✨ **Zen correspondence, quiet intimacy, respect for silence, focus on words, minimal design**

# RECOMMENDATION FRAMEWORK

**Choose Direction A if:**
- You want nostalgic, warm feeling
- Appeal to people who love vintage/analog
- Strong journal metaphor
- Comfortable with decorative elements

**Choose Direction B if:**
- You want romantic, exciting feeling
- Photo reveal as dramatic moment
- Appeal to romantic users
- Comfort with mystery/discovery

**Choose Direction C if:**
- You want zen, minimalist feeling
- Focus purely on the words
- Appeal to thoughtful users
- Preference for clean design
